How To Play

Hello and welcome to Picture Perfect! This is a desktop memory match game. Depending on whether you play alone or with your friends the goal of the game is to either...

1. Find all of the matches before the timer runs out in single player mode.

2. Or find the most matches in multiplayer mode. The player with the most matches once all of the cards have been flipped, or the timer runs out, wins the game!

Click the "play now" button to move to the selection screen.

Good luck and have fun!

Selection Instructions

1. Choose how many players are going to play, and enter your names.

2. Select your game play mode. (Each difficulty level will add more cards to the grid, and give you a little extra time.)

3. Click "play game" when you're ready to start the fun to take you do the game screen!

Game-Play Instructions

Click "start playing" to start the clock and enable the cards to be flipped!

Hint: Flip over 2 cards and see if they match. If they don't match, try to remember what pictures are where so you can come back and get a point when you do find their match!

The reset button will reset your score(s), and the timer, as well as shuffle the card grid.
